About Me

I am recent graduate from The Iron Yard Orlando. I was part of the front end engineering cohort from July 2016 to October 2016. During the program I learned HTML, CSS, Sass, jQuery, npm, git and React.

I am excited about building new web apps and learning new skills in the process. UX/UI is a newfound passion of mine that I am continuing to enhance. Being thrust into the developer community was fun, exciting, at times frustrating, but most of all: rewarding. It is truly amazing that an idea can be transformed into something the world can use.

In my prior jobs I was able to work with and manage teams, master multiple computer systems, and interact with clients. I am a burgeoning coder and feel my past experiences will boost my performance as a professional developer.

Budget Me

Previous Next

This was my final project from The Iron Yard, built with my class partner Lake Black. This project was made completely from scratch. We recognized a problem with people not being able to save money. One of the ways to help, was for them to build a budget.

Applying firebase authorization, users can login and play with the input ranges to see what budget suites their needs. Users are able to view what areas they may be spending too much money on and alternately view areas they can spend more in.

Changes are made in real time and users will be able to login to their profiles to see their previous balancing. Others features includes a loans pages, allowing users to shift interest rates and terms. A goals page, enabling the user to set a goal and see the breakdown of projected savings throughout the time. In addition, the app is optimized for mobile.

Visit Repo: https://github.com/lakeblack/final_budget_app

Created: October 2016

Built With: React, Firebase, Google Charts API
